We invite papers on analytics, machine learning (ML) and artificial intelligence (AI) from researchers, industry professionals, and students to showcase their novel and innovative work.

  • Editorial Team :
    • Prof Dr Dinesh Kumar, Professor, IIM Bangalore
    • Prof Dr Indranil Bose, Professor, IIM Ahmedabad
    • Prof Dr Rameshwar Dubey, Professor, Montpellier Business School,Reader, Doctoral School of Management Liverpool Business School
    • Prof Dr Chandan Majumder, Professor, JU
    • Prof Dr Suparna Dhar, Professor, NSHM Knowledge Campus Kolkata
    • Prof Dr Sanjay Goswami, Associate Professor, NSHM Knowledge Campus Kolkata

    Topics of interest for submission include, but are not limited to:

    • Analytics methods, tools & techniques:
      • Data collection and preprocessing for analytics and AI/ML
      • Data visualization for analytics
      • Machine learning
      • Deep learning
      • Social media analytics
      • Text, image and video analytics
    • Applications of analytics in business:
      • Business process development with analytics
      • Applications of analytics in cybersecurity, law and order, and governance
      • Application of analytics in disaster management
      • Application of analytics for societal development, education, health, etc.
      • Case studies on application of analytics

    You are invited to submit your concept papers, short research papers and full paper to the conference.

    IMPORTANT DATES:
    Submission deadline: 21st December, 2023 (Extended untill 26th December, 2023)
    Acceptance notification: 15 February, 2024
    Selected papers will be published by Springer in Communications in Computer and Information Science (CCIS) by Springer, indexed in SCOPUS, SCImago, DBLP, JST, etc. Best two papers will receive cash awards.

    Please follow the Author Guidelines: Click Here To Visit Springer Guidelines Page.
    Please submit your paper in the specified template.

    Please submit your paper at AGC2024 Submission Website on or before the submission dates.

    Note: If you do not get a confirmation mail with paper id within 24 hours of submission, please resubmit.
